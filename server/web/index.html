Avatari

Avatari is an Android app designed to interact with your fitbit to help make exercising fun

Find Out More

Avatari makes fitness fun again!

Avatari is a great way to get yourself motivated! You wont even remember you are exercising you are having so much fun!

By syncing with your FitBit, Avatari can track your progress and convert real world exercise into your players attributes. The more you exercise, the stronger your Avatar gets. Exercise more, grow your Avatar, level up and fight the bosses.
